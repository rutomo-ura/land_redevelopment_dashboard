Method And Governance

The public bundle omits owner names and internal notes. use_group is derived from county assessment usedesc; zoning must be checked for permissible use. ZIP rates use residential assessment parcels as the denominator. Neighborhood and Council assignments use authoritative City boundaries and parcel centroids.
