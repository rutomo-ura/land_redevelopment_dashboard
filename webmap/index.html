Analyst Notes

This app uses a residential-focused extract: parcels under 2 acres, removing road, rail, utility, public-service, industrial, commercial, and other non-residential anomaly categories. ZIP rates use all residential assessment parcels as the denominator. Neighborhood counts are EPP-linked concentrations, so treat them as cluster signals rather than final neighborhood-wide vacancy rates.
